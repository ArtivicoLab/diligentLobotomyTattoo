COVID-19 Safety Protocols

Your safety and our artists' safety is our top priority. We have comprehensive protocols in place.

Face Mask Policy

All clients must wear a face mask at all times if you are not fully vaccinated. This helps protect everyone in our studio environment.

Hand Sanitization

Practice hand washing/sanitizing before and after any paperwork or transactions. Sanitizer stations are available throughout the shop.

Exposure Policy

Please postpone your appointment if you or anyone you have come in contact with may have been exposed to COVID-19.

No Additional Guests

For safety and social distancing, please do not bring additional guests to your appointment unless absolutely necessary.

Luxury Aftercare

Protect your investment with our premium aftercare program designed for vibrant, long-lasting horror art

1

Immediate Care (0-24 hours)

Remove bandage after 2-4 hours. Gently wash with antibacterial soap and pat dry. Apply thin layer of our premium healing balm.

2

Healing Phase (1-14 days)

Wash 2-3 times daily. Apply our luxury moisturizer. Avoid sun, swimming, and tight clothing. Colors will appear vibrant during this phase.

3

Settling Phase (2-4 weeks)

Continue moisturizing daily. Your tattoo will settle into its final colors. Use our UV protection formula when in sunlight.

4

Long-term Care

Maintain with our luxury tattoo care line. Schedule annual touch-ups to keep your horror art vibrant and stunning.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our luxury horror tattoo experience

How long does a typical horror tattoo session take?

Sessions typically range from 2-8 hours depending on the complexity and size of your horror design. Our vibrant zombie portraits usually take 4-6 hours for optimal color saturation and detail work.

What makes DLT's color work different?

We use premium imported inks and advanced color theory techniques to create vibrant, long-lasting colors that maintain their intensity. Our artists are specially trained in horror color palettes that pop against skin tones.

Do you offer payment plans for larger pieces?

Yes! We offer luxury payment plans for pieces over $1000. Split your investment into 3-6 monthly payments with no interest for qualified clients.

How do I prepare for my horror tattoo session?

Get plenty of sleep, eat a good meal, stay hydrated, and avoid alcohol for 24 hours prior. Wear comfortable clothing that allows easy access to the tattoo area.

What's included in the luxury aftercare package?

Our luxury package includes premium healing balm, color-protect moisturizer, UV shield, detailed care instructions, and unlimited aftercare consultations for the first month.
